Welcome to UOW Doctors

This is a website owned by the University of Wollongong medical facility. The purpose of this website is to help students find the Docotrs they need to fix an appointment.

If you know the Docotr that you require you can search that particular doctor on the search menu below. Press the "Search Doctor" key below to directly head over there.

If you don't know a doctor or what doctor you require, please click the "Enter your details". There you will be asked to provide details about the problem you have so that we find a suitable doctor for you.

Once you submit your infomration you will require to login into your account. To login into your account enter your SOLS details to login.
